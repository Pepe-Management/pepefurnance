Policy & Disclosures

Purpose

The PEPE Furnace project converts YouTube ad revenue into PEPE and sends it to the Ethereum burn address. All activity is public, on-chain, and recorded in the Burn Log.

Monetization & Eligibility

  • We are building the YouTube channel to qualify for the YouTube Partner Program (YPP).
  • Eligibility targets: 1,000 subscribers and either 4,000+ public watch hours (12 months) or 10M+ Shorts views (90 days).
  • A linked Google AdSense account is required for payouts. Until YPP approval, burns may be small/irregular and may include personal contributions.

Money Flow

  1. YouTube earnings → AdSense → bank account.
  2. Exchange purchase of PEPE (fees disclosed in Burn Log notes).
  3. PEPE → Collection wallet → 0x000000000000000000000000000000000000dEaD.
  4. Every burn TX and amount is logged in the Burn Log.

Security & Transparency

  • Collection wallet: 0x9FA6278aF6E20aA8fBBF1F347eDD5BF9B1f7032F.
  • Withdrawals from exchanges are whitelisted to the collection wallet only.
  • All tx hashes are posted; anyone can verify on Etherscan.

Disclaimers

  • $PEPE has no association with Matt Furie or Pepe the Frog. This is a fan/community project.
  • $PEPE is a meme coin with no intrinsic value or expectation of financial return. No promises, no investment advice.
  • Burn schedule is subject to YouTube monetization eligibility and payout timing.
