Burn Log Wallets Policy Roadmap

Roadmap

Stage 1 — Channel Growth (Weeks 1–12)

  • Publish 3–7 Shorts per week + 1 weekly recap video.
  • Targets: 1,000 subscribers and either 4,000 public watch hours (12 months) or 10M Shorts views (90 days).
  • Apply to the YouTube Partner Program and link Google AdSense once eligible.

Stage 2 — Monetization Live

  • AdSense payouts received monthly; batch into weekly PEPE buys.
  • Withdraw PEPE to collection wallet and execute weekly burns.
  • Post every tx hash + amounts in the Burn Log.

Stage 3 — Automation & Reporting

  • No-code/Light script to: confirm fiat balance → buy PEPE → withdraw → prep burn tx.
  • CSV export + changelog in repo for full transparency.
  • (Optional) Gnosis Safe multi-sig for collection wallet; Dune dashboard for totals.

Stage 4 — Community & Scale

  • Collabs with meme/crypto channels; livestream “burn nights”.
  • Community requests drive feature priorities (issues in the GitHub repo).

Note: Burn cadence depends on YouTube eligibility and payout timing until YPP is approved.
